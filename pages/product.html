Products

In the Products section of our website, farmers can easily find useful tools and ideas that are simple to use and helpful in their farms and fields. In addition, we have also included books below that farmers should read to gain more knowledge and improve their farming practices.

Spray
Farm Spray
  • Add lemon essential oil and dish soap to water in a spray bottle. Shake and spray on crops.
  • Safe for crops, animals, and humans (when used as directed)
  • Easy-to-use nozzles for even spraying
  • Fast-acting and long-lasting results
  • Available in various sizes for home gardens to large farms
Cage
Animal Cage
  • Use wooden frames and wire mesh for durable structure
  • Add doors for easy feeding and cleaning
  • Include a watering and feeding system
  • Ensure proper ventilation and protection from predators
  • Ideal for chickens, rabbits, and small livestock
Auto Water System
Automatic Watering
  • Use PVC pipes and a water tank
  • Install a timer to control water flow
  • Connect to drip lines or sprinklers
  • Save water and labor with automated irrigation
  • Great for vegetable gardens and greenhouses
Soil
Soil Improvement
  • Add compost and organic manure
  • Rotate crops to maintain soil nutrients
  • Use cover crops to prevent erosion
  • Check pH level and adjust with lime or sulfur
  • Enhance drainage with sand or organic matter
Tools
Farming Tools
  • Essential tools: shovel, hoe, rake, watering can
  • Gloves for safety and comfort
  • Pruning tools for plant maintenance
  • Wheelbarrow for transporting soil or crops
  • Durable, rust-resistant material for long life
Cart
Farm Cart
  • Build with wood planks for frame
  • Use sturdy wheels for smooth movement
  • Add a handle for easy pulling or pushing
  • Ideal for transporting tools, soil, and crops
  • Lightweight and farm-ready design
