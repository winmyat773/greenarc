We Believe in Greener Living

We are going to guide how sailors should respond and manage various things during stormy weather and heavy rainfall. We will share information about seasonal fruit cultivation, various planting techniques, and other topics related to agriculture. We will also share information about how the market works, so that crop prices don’t drop because some farmers don’t understand it well. Our website also shares how to stay strong and not lose hope when pests attack crops or when storms damage farms, and how to recover and fix things afterward.
